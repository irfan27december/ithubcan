About IT HUB

IT Hub Canberra Pty Ltd. was established in 2016 with the following goals:

  • Education and hands-on lab training on various vendor-oriented
  • Professional Certifications
  • Systems, Networking & Integration and consultation for SMEs
  • Web Development
  • IT Infrastructure Monitoring & Support for SMEs
  • Helping local communities with job-oriented training programs, offering job opportunities with our clients where possible.
  • Job-guaranteed programs (with conditions for enrollment).

Our Values

  • Understand and fully satisfy our customer’s requirements
  • Provide courteous, reliable, and cost-effective service, executed by professional staff
  • Continuously monitor and improve the effectiveness of the quality of the services provided

Our Services

Our Clients

  • Within a short period of service in the industry, we managed to deliver our services to clients such as NSW Police, NSW Dept. of Community Services, Qantas Airways, Dept. of Human Services in Canberra, IPD Group in Sydney, Australian Taxation Office Canberra, and NSW Health.
  • In addition to that, hundreds of individual customers have enrolled with us for our hands-on lab and job-oriented training program.
